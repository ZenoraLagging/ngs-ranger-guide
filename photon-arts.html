Example Rifle Rotation

Weak Bullet
>
Fallen Impact
(if Launcher multi-weapon)
>
Launcher WA
(if Launcher multi-weapon)
>
Revolt Aim
(when positioned correctly)
>
Rifle Grenade
(when out of PP; grenade > mine if the mine can't hit the weakspot)

Otherwise, here's a tl;dr:

Legend (Rifle's optimal range damage boost)

Default | Short range (+10% damage) | Optimal range (+20% damage)



Weak Bullet
First priority is to refresh and/or apply Weak Bullet, +20% damage debuff is no joke.
Raging Shot
Use tapped version on small enemies for
~239.09 | ~263 | ~286.91 DPS for 22 PP over ~1.1 seconds

Charged version on big enemies with big hitboxes for up to
~283.5 | ~311.85 | ~340.2 DPS for 22 PP over ~1.2 seconds
Step Counter

Use step counter against aggressive enemies/lingering hitboxes for
~272 | ~299.2 | ~326.4 DPS,
or slide dodge with Rifle's Weapon Action to refresh Rifle Grenadier for ~369.23 Grenade DPS, or ~461.54 Mine DPS
Revolt Aim
Use Revolt Aim if an enemy briefly stands still for 351.6 DPS, costing 20 PP over ~2 seconds.
This PA always hits for maximum damage and bypasses through enemy hitboxes.

Use this PA to mob against scattered mobs, otherwise use Divine Impact.

Deals a mere
100 | 110 | 120 DPS for 10 hits costing 28 PP over 3 seconds, or
212 | 233 | 255 DPS for 100 hits costing 105 PP over 13 seconds.
Avoid using this PA at all costs.

Slides towards a direction (default: backwards) while quickly firing 4 shots.
With Rifle Grenadier, holding weapon action with a directional input throws a grenade, otherwise it installs a mine at your current position.

Rifle Photon Blast

Photon Blast
Power 3250
Frames 275

Fires an overhead satellite beam that decimates foes.
As this attacks hits from top-down, this can be blocked by their heads or other hitboxes.
Launcher Photon Blast is better as it's more reliable over Rifle's.

Raging Shot

Uncharged Charged
PP Cost 22
Power 263 335 (3 hits)
Frames 66 72

Fires a exploding bullet. Charging the PA turns it into a piercing bullet.
Uncharged version has better tracking against mobile bosses, but has worse PP efficiency.

Homing Dart

Bullet (Hitting once) Bullet (Landing on same target after first)
PP Cost 24
Power 240 ??

Fires a homing bullet that can track up to 10 enemies or parts. Deals reduced damage if landing 2 or more bullets on the same target.

Blaze Shot

Initial hit Channeled bullets
PP Cost 20
Power 26
Frames 86 180 (10 shots)

Dodges enemy attacks on start-up, before going into a maneuverable stance, shooting bullets while held.

Revolt Aim

PP Cost 20
Power 660
Frames 133

Fires lasers hitting up to 5 times at a fixed distance.
Main DPS skill on stationary targets, but has fixed range, and locks you in animation while firing.
Can be step-canceled before the shot happens.

Example Launcher Rotation

On a sandbag, do the following:


Weak Bullet
>
Fallen Impact
>
Launcher WA
>
Launcher Photon Blast
(if available)
>
Fear Eraser
(Only with another Ranger. You will be forced to cancel Fear Eraser after 10 seconds for Weak Bullet upkeep.)

Otherwise, here's a tl;dr:



Weak Bullet
First priority is to refresh and/or apply Weak Bullet, +20% damage debuff is no joke.
Launcher Weapon Action
(Sticky Bomb)
Use at the start of a rotation to sustain PP; detonates after 10 seconds, exploding and refunding up to 100 PP based on hits dealt
(max: 10 launcher hits)
Launcher Charged Normals
Use to restore PP when necessary;
restores 26 PP if you choke (hold forward) the shots
(takes 1s to charge, 0.5s with the skill)
Step dodge counter is 310 DPS for reference.
Fallen Impact
Use to set-up damage on stationary/exposed weakpoints for 341 (actual 133) DPS for 25 PP, takes ~1.2 seconds to fire, ~4 seconds to finish.
Multiple Launch
Track fast-moving enemies, or hard-to-reach hitboxes for up to 239 DPS, costing 30 PP over ~3s.
This PA has insane tracking, but can't be canceled after the 4th shot.
Divine Impact
Use this PA to mob against grouped mobs, otherwise use Divine Impact.
Fear Eraser
Sustained DPS option. Must hold the PA for at least 16 seconds, costing 150 PP, to outdps Revolt Aim (350 Laser DPS vs 352 Revolt Aim DPS).
You can't be interrupted while channeling, but can be stunned/frozen/etc.

Launcher Weapon Action

Sticky Bomb
PP Gain 50 + (5 x # of hits [max 10]) PP, max 100 PP
Cooldown 60s
Power 120
Frames 40f, 608f (Sticky timer)

Throws a sticky bomb that deals no damage on initial contact.
Attacking the stickied enemy activates it, exploding 10 seconds after.
The grenade restores more PP (max 100) based on the number of launcher hits
(5 PP x # of hits [max 10 hits])
Learning Cluster Bomb Advanced reduces the cooldown by 30s.
Shows a visual indicator when it's off cooldown.

Launcher Photon Blast

Photon Blast
Power 2660
Frames 173

Fires a powerful grenade that rapidly expands upon impact, ending with a powerful blast.
Unlike Rifle, this attack doesn't lock you in animation!
One of the best sandbag DPS attacks when its available.

Fallen Impact

PP Cost 25
Power 500
Frames 89f (firing animation), 108f (delayed hit)

Fires a mortar above enemies. Firing multiple mortars before it falls down will remove the previous one.

Fear Eraser

Tapped Laser Tick Stage 2
PP Cost 35 + 10 every second
Power 400 200 1600
Frames 226 30 384

Channels a powerful laser from your launcher. Holding the PA will drain PP continuously, adding a secondary attack after a certain period of time.
PP cost reduction doesn't affect the laser's channeling PP cost.

Multi Launch

PP Cost 30
Power 1010
Frames 258

Fires a stream of rockets towards the target, before finishing with a powerful blast.
You can move while shooting.
Goes through all objects except for walls.
Has great PP efficiency, and is dash-cancelable up until the 6th shot.

Divine Impact

Uncharged Charged
PP Cost 28
Power 285 570
Frames 54 140

Fires a blast in front of you.
Charging this attack fires a powerful grenade instead.
