Main Class only

Weak Bullet Reinforce

Level 1
Damage 125%
Mark Duration 15
Required Hit Count 100 hits

When you & other players hit parts marked with Weak Bullet 100 times, the marker becomes reinforced, improving damage while resetting the skill's duration.
Reinforced markers glow purple and overrides the +20% bonus with the +25%.

It's important that you re-apply Weak Bullet as it preserves both the hitcount (allowing solo Rangers to reach purple marker), and the duration of the purple marker itself. It'll disappear if it wears off though.

Rifle only

Rifle Grenadier

Level 1
Trap Power 700%
Grenade Power 560%
Cooldown 30s

Holding Rifle's Weapon Action adds an additional grenade attack.
Input a direction at the same time to throw the grenade, or input nothing to place a mine.
Shows a visual indicator when it comes off cooldown.

Rifle only

Rifle Slide Shot Advance

With an Assault Rifle equipped, Weapon Actions will trigger an invulnerability period.

Rifle only

Spread Shot

Expend a gauge built up by Assault Rifle attacks to fire a special close range shotgun blast.

Main Class only Rifle only

Spread Shot Wide Range

Modifies Spread Shot into an AoE attack.

Rifle only

Spread Shot Quick Escape

Triggers an invulnerability period when you use Spread Shot.
Inputting a direction will also extend the distance of the evasive action.
