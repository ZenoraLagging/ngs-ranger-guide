Weapon Overview

Assault Rifles

Mid-range mobile assaulter

Rifle is a middle-range specialist that relies on staying within optimal distance for maximum effectiveness. Allows access to Weak Bullet, which places a marker on enemies for +20% more damage.
Because of their fast animations, they are best at dealing with enemy aggro.

Optimal Distance Mechanic Explained Sample Rifle Rotation

Grenade Launchers

Slow, powerful demolitionist

Launchers are the heavy-duty damage dealers that relies on committing attacks for maximum DPS.
Relies on good PP management to sustain their main damage option, Fear Eraser
Because of their sluggish animations, they shine best during sandbag situations.

Photon Arts

Assault Rifles

Revolt Aim

New!
Main sandbag DPS

Fires lasers hitting up to 5 times at a fixed distance.
Difficult to use, but has one of the highest DPS on stationary targets.

Raging Shot

DPS Skill

Shoots a explosive round. Can be charged to pierce through enemies.
Uncharged version has better tracking on mobile enemies, but has worse PP efficiency.

Homing Dart

AoE Skill

Locks up to 10 targets, before firing off homing shots.

Blaze

Evasion Skill
Repositioning Tool

Twirls around targets before firing a powerful shot. Can be held to go into a movable shooting stance.
Great for kiting targets (and reinforcing Weak Bullet while soloing), but loses to other PAs in a sandbag situation.

Grenade Launchers

Divine Impact

New!
Main AoE Skill

Fires a blast in front of you.
Charging this attack fires a powerful grenade instead

Fallen Impact

Situational

Fires a delayed mortar that lands after 2 seconds. Only one can exist at any time.

Fear Eraser

Main DPS Skill

Channels a powerful laser that continuously drains PP while held.
Adds a secondary attack when held for 3 seconds or longer.

Multi-Launch

Hitscan

Fires a barrage of rockets that has great tracking against enemies.
Has great PP efficiency, and can be dash-cancelled up until the 6th shot.

Gearing Up

Min-max

Cinquem

Currently the best weapon as of this writing; even at level 1 potential it beats Relik at level 4, while offering mostly unconditional bonuses like better Restasign recovery and PP reduction! The only issue is that leveling up the potentials eats Photon Scales, easily reaching up to 1250 total scales to unlock level 4 unless you use one of those potential skips.

Virtuoso Unit
Fixa Attack recommended

Strong

Relik Series

Formerly the best weapon before Cinquem; rewards great positioning and avoiding attacks as it boasts the highest base attack of all the weapons along with a 25% critical rate increase while above 80% HP with virtually no downsides with how easy NGS healing is.

Harmonious Unit
Fixa Termina recommended

Budget

Greaga

The go-to weapon for Bossing but is outclassed by Relik due to the difference in base attack. Still holds the 2nd highest base attack and is a great budget to invest in until one obtains the Relik Series.

Fighting Spirit Unit
Fixa Attack / Fixa Fatale recommended


Niche

Gothica

Shares the lowest base attack of all the current weapons along with the Fivla Series making it unable to compete against the rest. It's sole existence is to be used as a PP Battery as a sub weapon although even that is pretty underwhelming. Only available as a Rifle.

Reinvigorating Unit

Meh.

Fivla

Shares the lowest base attack and potency compared to the Gothica series. Potential is highly reliant on critical RNG and the rifle being unavailable makes it all the more underwhelming to invest in.

Class Combos

Recommended

Force

PP Battery
Usable Weapon(s)
Open Skill Tree

Offers great PP economy by boosting your natural PP regen through passives and actives
(150% passively, multiplies by 600% when activating PP Convert)
Note that you can't regen PP while using PAs.
Also allows rod, talis, and tech access to exploit element weaknesses for additional stuns.

Gunner

PP Sustain
Usable Weapon(s)
Open Skill Tree

Offers great PP recovery by improving PP regen from attacking enemies
(144% attack PP regen, 120% against bosses, includes grenades)
Gunner sub can be used against bosses, but Force & Fighter would perform better if you have issues managing grenades.
TMGs can be ignored as they don't offer much compared to Rifle/Launcher.
