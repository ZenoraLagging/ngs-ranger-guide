Summarized Analysis

Here's how each subclasses fare in the current meta!


Warning! The skill trees assumes that you'll be using them as pure subclasses. Adjust skill points accordingly if that's not the case.

Recommended

Force

PP Battery
Usable Weapon(s)
Open Skill Tree

Offers great PP economy by boosting your natural PP regen through passives and actives
(150% passively, multiplies by 600% when activating PP Convert)
Note that you can't regen PP while using PAs.
Also allows rod, talis, and tech access to exploit element weaknesses for additional stuns.

Gunner

PP Sustain
Usable Weapon(s)
Open Skill Tree

Offers great PP recovery by improving PP regen from attacking enemies
(144% attack PP regen, 120% against bosses, includes grenades)
Gunner sub can be used against bosses, but Force & Fighter would perform better if you have issues managing grenades.
TMGs can be ignored as they don't offer much compared to Rifle/Launcher.

Viable

Fighter

Bossing
Usable Weapon(s)
Open Skill Tree

Improves Ranger's single target against downed bosses.
(105% damage & 125% PP Regen against downed bosses)
The conditional passives makes Fighter exclusively for bosses.
Also allows TD, DS & Knuckles access for different playstyles.

Braver

Burst
Usable Weapon(s)
Open Skill Tree

Improves Ranger's HP self-sustain against bosses.
(110% better Resta Sign effect, recover 10% HP when doing a breakfall)
Brave Combat enables bursting strats, but requires a Braver weapon to use, and the HP&PP regen when finishing is main class only, which diminishes its usefulness.
Bow has better burst damage & stronger counters via Weapon Action, but Ranger's weapons have better consistency.
Allows Katana & Bow usage for different playstyles.

Hunter

Defensive
Usable Weapon(s)
Open Skill Tree

Improves Ranger's defense by reducing damage taken, or granting super armor.
(10% passive reduction, 25% reduction & super armor from Hunter Physique)
As Ranger is a ranged class, Hunter isn't required but nice to have.
Allows Swords, Wired Lance, and Partizan access.

Situational

Bouncer

Part Destroyer Specialist
Usable Weapon(s)
Open Skill Tree

Grants faster Down Factory towards enemies while offering benefits related to parts broken
(110% faster build-up, +1% damage & +2% damage reduction per parts broke (max stack: 3)).
Their weapons conflict with Ranger's playstyle and doesn't offer much.
A highly situational subclass that offers little benefits (Besides Techter).
Allows tech access.

Techter

Photon Blast Charger
Usable Weapon(s)
Open Skill Tree

Grants faster Photon Blast when nearby bosses or exploiting elemental weaknesses.
(110% when matching weaknesses, 110% multiplicative when nearby bosses).
A highly situational subclass that offers almost no benefits over other choices.
Allows Wand, Talis, and tech access.
