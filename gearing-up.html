Current Line-up

Min-max
Recommended for Multi-Weapon

Resurge Series

Able to multi-weapon both rifle and launcher, has crit rate bonus making it the strongest out of the bunch in terms of raw output and versatility.

Dynamo Unit

Viable

Foursis Series

You want to play tanky during Gigantix period, this helps with -% damage reduction when at max HP. Paired with NGS gear it's pretty bulky, but doesn't reward players who can dodge consistently. Middle of the pack damage, but competitive nonetheless with Fixa prefixes.

Bastion Unit

Viable

Vialto Series

PP recovery both active and natural by 10%. This affects Launcher's WA, which is good because it can multi-weapon with Talis, which also affects its PP regen options as well - making Fear Eraser more sustainable. If you invested in it already its best not to go with the Resurge Series and just stick with the Talis combo.

Meditation Unit

Budget
Recommended for Multi-Weapon

Terseus Series

3 star version of Resurge which uses your defense as a conversion for crit rate. Very good if you use 4 star NGS units which have more defense than anything PSO2 can offer. Cheaper alternative and is somewhat overlooked if you're trying to get Fixa prefixes on a weapon.

Defensive Formation

New Game Bonus

Lightweaver Cras Series

PSO2 players with a fully kitted Lightweaver Cras Series can skip starting NGS weapons, as their weapon provides up to 8%+ power and other bonuses from abilities like Raising Pursuit, which grants +50% more PA/Tech damage for 5 seconds when hitting with any PA/Tech. Fixa prefixes on 3* weapons roughly matches Cras' power (before additional bonuses) and is generally cheap, so don't get too attached to your weapon.
