Multi-Weapon System

Should I do it?

Ranger greatly benefits from having a Rifle + Launcher (or Launcher + Rifle, it doesn't matter which) as their multi-weapon combo as it opens up lots of option, such as having Weak Bullet access on a Launcher, seamlessly transitioning between Rifle & Launcher PAs, etc.
For other weapons, it depends on your playstyle and use cases.

Weapons

Min-max

Relik Series

Undisputed the best series and weapon as of the current released weapons. Heavily rewards great positioning and avoiding attacks as it boasts the highest base attack of all the weapons along with a 25% critical rate increase while above 80% HP with virtually no downsides with how easy NGS healing is.

Harmonious Unit
Fixa Termina recommended

Budget

Greaga

The go-to weapon for Bossing but is outclassed by Relik due to the difference in base attack. Still holds the 2nd highest base attack and is a great budget to invest in until one obtains the Relik Series.

Fighting Spirit Unit
Fixa Attack / Fixa Fatale recommended


Niche

Gothica

Shares the lowest base attack of all the current weapons along with the Fivla Series making it unable to compete against the rest. It's sole existence is to be used as a PP Battery as a sub weapon although even that is pretty underwhelming. Only available as a Rifle.

Reinvigorating Unit

Meh.

Fivla

Shares the lowest base attack and potency compared to the Gothica series. Potential is highly reliant on critical RNG and the rifle being unavailable makes it all the more underwhelming to invest in.
